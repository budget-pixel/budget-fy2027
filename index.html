Methodology & Disclaimer

Parcel data is sourced from the Florida GIO statewide dataset. This calculator uses Non-School Taxable Value and the last approved Walton County millage rate of 3.519 mills to estimate the county portion of a user's tax bill. The service breakdown allocates the estimated tax bill proportionally across Walton County's property tax revenue allocations.

Some listed services are shown as fully funded by property tax revenue, while others represent only the portion of that service funded by property tax revenue. Some services generate other revenues from charges for services, fees, or other sources; the allocations shown are net of those other revenues and represent the property tax revenue portion.

This calculator is provided for informational and educational purposes only. Results are estimates and should not be considered an official determination of taxes. Actual tax amounts may vary based on property classification, exemptions, assessment limitations, taxable value determinations, millage rates, voter-approved levies, and other factors. Users should view official records from the Walton County Property Appraiser for parcel-specific tax information.
