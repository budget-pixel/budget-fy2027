Office of Management and Budget

Statement of Function The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) plays a pivotal role in Walton County by providing comprehensive financial and administrative support to the Board of County Commissioners (BCC). The department is responsible for overseeing all funds authorized to ensure their proper and legal utilization, and offers advice and guidance to department and division heads. Within the department, key functions include preparing and monitoring the annual operating and capital budget, overseeing grant budgets and reporting, and maintaining the inventory of capital assets. By diligently executing these responsibilities, the OMB contributes significantly to the County’s fiscal responsibility and overall operational efficiency, in alignment with BCC policies and regulatory standards.
