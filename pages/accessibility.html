Public Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Walton County is committed to providing access to online budget information for all residents, including people with disabilities. This budget website is designed to support accessibility and usability consistent with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

If you experience difficulty accessing any content, table, chart, document, or feature on this website, please contact Walton County so the information can be provided in an accessible format.
