Introduction and Overview

Strategic Initiatives

Introduction The Board of County Commissioners, with input from the County Administrator, Chief Financial Officer, County Attorney, county departments, and the public, establishes the County’s strategic initiatives. These initiatives represent broad, guiding areas of focus that steer Walton County toward a prosperous and resilient future. At their foundation is our Mission Statement , which defines our purpose: to serve and protect our community while fostering economic growth and preserving natural resources. Complementing this is our Vision Statement , setting our aspirational goal—to make Walton County an exceptional place to live, work, and enjoy life. Our Core Values , including transparency, integrity, and accountability, act as guiding principles for every decision and action. To translate these values and aspirations into tangible outcomes, the County has identified Strategic Initiatives —broad priorities such as sustainable infrastructure and safe communities. These initiatives highlight key areas for strategic focus and investment, helping guide the County’s actions. To support these broad initiatives, each department creates departmental goals aligned to a Strategic Initiative. Departments then establish specific, measurable objectives and performance measures to assess their effectiveness and efficiency. This structured yet flexible approach ensures alignment across county operations, accountability to residents, and progress toward achieving our shared vision. Core Values Strategic Initiatives The Strategic Initiatives in Walton County represent broad, guiding areas essential for translating our mission—creating a safe, sustainable community—into meaningful action. These initiatives inform policy-making, resource allocation, and decision-making, supporting comprehensive community growth and development. Covering areas such as infrastructure, environmental stewardship, and community safety, the initiatives ensure county efforts are coordinated and responsive to citizens’ needs. Each department develops goals aligned directly with these strategic initiatives, clearly identifying the initiative through the corresponding Roman numeral indicated on their departmental performance measure reports. Departments then establish specific, measurable objectives and related performance measures to track their progress, ensuring transparency and accountability. Through this intentional approach, Walton County effectively addresses current challenges and positions itself strategically for future opportunities, supporting our vision of being a premier community to live, work, and thrive.
