Human Resources

Statement of Function The Walton County Department of Human Resources provides centralized personnel services for all Walton County BCC departments. We work with departments to identify and assist with workforce issues, including recruitment, selection, performance management, standardizing discipline policies, employee development and knowledge transfer, managing workers’ compensation benefits, and providing ongoing evaluation of workplace policies and procedures. We manage benefits for the Walton County BCC, all constitutional offices, and retirees, including health, dental, vision, life insurance, LTD, optional coverages, and the Florida Retirement System. Furthermore, we oversee ADA compliance issues, including countywide ADA inspections, software conformity, and we respond to ADA concerns brought to the attention of the county. We currently are staffed with 13 full-time employees.
