Planning

Statement of Function The Walton County Planning & Development Services Department serves as staff and provides professional land use planning advice to the Board of County Commissioners. The Department is responsible for implementing, maintaining, and updating the Walton County Comprehensive Plan and Land Development Code, concurrency management, including implementation of the Mobility Plan, Floodplain Management, and management of growth through consistent residential and non-residential development review in unincorporated Walton County to ensure that a proposed development or request is in compliance with the adopted codes, policies, regulations and standards of the County and of other public and quasi-public agencies. The Department also staffs the Planning Commission, the Zoning Board of Adjustments, the Design Review Board, and the Affordable Housing Committee.
