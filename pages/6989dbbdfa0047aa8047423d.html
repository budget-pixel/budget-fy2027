Emergency Management

Statement of Function The Emergency Management Department is responsible for all aspects of disaster management 24 hours a day/seven days a week. We take a “Whole Community Approach’ which is a collaborative effort involving government agencies, non-profit organizations, businesses, and community members. The Emergency Operations Center (EOC) serves as the central hub for communications, command, and coordination for disasters in Walton County and in the EOC we have processes for streamlined resource management, tactical operations, and information coordination with the public and our responding community partners.
