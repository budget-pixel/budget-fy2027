Engineering Department

Statement of Function The primary function of the County Engineering Department is to manage the design and construction of Walton County infrastructure projects. Additional departmental duties include, but are not limited to, providing capital improvement design and CEI project management (consultants), providing capital improvement construction project management (contractors), traffic operations, right-of-way permitting, surveying, infrastructure (FDOT) grant writing/applications and administration, right-of-way review for development order applications for the Planning Department, plat review for the Planning Department applications, driveway connection permitting to County facilities within municipal boundaries and utility/engineering oversight for the sewer system at the Northwest Florida Commerce Park at Mossy Head. The Engineering Department works with other internal County departments and other County agencies in project management and provides support on grant funded infrastructure improvement projects while coordinating with OMB on grant administration. Project management of County funded capital improvement projects, including in-house projects, is performed by Department staff. Coordination with utility companies, permitting agencies and other project associated parties is facilitated by department staff. The Department provides long range infrastructure programming and coordinates with regional organizations such as the Okaloosa-Walton Transportation Planning Organization. Staff coordinates with Florida Department of Transportation (FDOT), Florida Department of Environmental Protection (FDEP), Northwest Florida Water Management District (NWFWMD), United States Army Corps of Engineers (USACOE) and other State and Federal Agencies that hold regulatory jurisdiction over County projects.
