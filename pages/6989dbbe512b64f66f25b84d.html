Department Information

Public Works

Statement of Function The Public Works Department provides services related to infrastructure maintenance, repair, and construction that will enhance the quality of life for Walton County citizens and visitors. We go to great lengths to stay abreast of what the concerns of our citizens are, while being mindful of the available taxpayer funds. We diligently consider every infrastructure improvement plan to ensure we are providing the best solutions that will provide a positive, long-term impact.
