Code Compliance

Statement of Function The primary function of Code Compliance is to uphold and enhance the aesthetics, property values, health and safety, and overall quality of life for the residents and visitors of Walton County. This is achieved through the fair, consistent, and equitable enforcement of codes, regulations, and ordinances. By diligently ensuring adherence to established standards, Code Compliance plays a vital role in preserving the visual appeal and structural integrity of properties, safeguarding public health and safety, and fostering a harmonious and thriving community environment. Through their dedicated efforts, the department strives to maintain a high standard of living and a welcoming atmosphere for all who live in or visit Walton County.
