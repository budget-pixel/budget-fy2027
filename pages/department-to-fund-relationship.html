General Fund

Primary operating fund for countywide services, constitutional offices, and programs not accounted for in a special revenue fund.

Sheriff Fund

Accounts for the Sheriff’s Office and other public safety costs historically shown in the Fine & Forfeiture Fund.

Special Revenue Funds

Track restricted resources such as transportation, tourist development, solid waste, mosquito control, and grant-supported programs.

Capital Projects

Separates major infrastructure, facility, technology, and equipment investments from regular operating costs.
